The Social-Hub requires an application engineered that would be able to bring the most popular and frequently used social media applications all into one application. The idea is to be able to authenticate all these applications by one click with the application and to be viewed in one Interface. A unique single access password to secure all your social networking accounts . Users can upload photos ,videos,Gifs and also be able to view comments ,notifications and updates from their favourite social media application all within the app. Title: Social networking camera/video application.

The social-Hub is a company which found a gap in the social media market and have decided to create an app where its users would be able most or all of their popular social media applications all in one place and can be able to post videos and pictures taken with their smart devices onto multiple social media applications, all at the same time.
